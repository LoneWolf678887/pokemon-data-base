Johto

Subtitle: The Johto Journeys / Johto League Champions / Master Quest
Region: Johto
Companions: Misty, Brock
Goal: Win the Johto League
Result: Top 8

Pokémon Caught:

1. Heracross – Strong battler, later sent to Oak’s lab.
2. Chikorita → Bayleef – Often affectionate toward Ash.
3. Cyndaquil → Quilava – Evolved in Sinnoh later.
4. Totodile – Playful personality.
5. Noctowl (Shiny) – First shiny Pokémon caught by Ash.
6. Phanpy – Hatched from an egg, later evolved in Hoenn.
